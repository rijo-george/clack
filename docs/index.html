Features

Your keyboard sounds
incredible.

🔈

Sound Packs

Cherry Blue, Typewriter, Thock, Bubble Pop. Each pack is carefully recorded from real keyboards for authentic feel.

🎜

Volume Control

Dial in the perfect level. From subtle background texture to full clicky immersion. Adjust anytime from the menu bar.

☰

Menu Bar Toggle

Lives in your menu bar, out of the way. One click to switch sound packs, adjust volume, or pause. No dock icon.

🔒

Accessibility Permission

Uses macOS Accessibility to listen for keystrokes. No keylogging, no network calls. Your typing stays private.

⚡

Lightweight

Near-zero CPU usage. Sounds play instantly on each keystroke with no perceptible delay. Battery-friendly.

🎨

Launch at Login

Set it once, forget it. Clack starts with your Mac and remembers your sound pack and volume preferences.

How It Works

Three steps to
clicky bliss.

1

Launch Clack

Open the app and grant Accessibility permission. Clack appears in your menu bar, ready to go.

2

Pick a sound pack

Cherry Blue for that classic mechanical feel. Typewriter for vintage vibes. Thock for deep, satisfying keystrokes.

3

Type away

Every keystroke plays your chosen sound. Adjust volume, switch packs, or toggle on and off from the menu bar anytime.
